Objectives and Intro Prep Posting

Part 1: Reread the description of what to include in the Objectives section

I will rank the Objectives in the following order:

  • Sample 2: Healthcare
  • Sample 1: Safe house
  • Sample 3: Website redesign

For sample 2, it has very good parallelism together with details. One thing that could be improved is that the writer can move the repeated part to the beginning of each section.

For sample 1, those objectives are not 100 per cent parallel. And the “non-functional requirements” section should start with “the application must”.

For sample 3, the objectives does not have all the sections required and they are not parallel.

Part 2: Review the changes between the 2 versions of the Objectives in the SOW documents below.

  • Sample 1: Safe house
    • Compare the final version with version 0.1, the final version is more parallel by putting verb at the beginning of each sentence instead of noun. Also, final version has more details.
  • Sample 2: Healthcare
    • One obvious change is much more details in the final version. At the same time, final version has more statistics that helps to measure the quality.

Part 3: Reread the description of what to include in your SOW Introduction

  • Potential mentor group
  • Potential mentee group

  • Exptected features of the final project
