5 Tips for Cleaning for Fall

Identify the process in an introduction

  • Define the process.
    • Clean the house for fall
  • Identify purpose or goal.
    • Clean the house and arrange your space for fall
  • Optional: Identify the intended audience and the purpose.
    • House owners
  • Optional: Explain background needed by the intended audience.
    • Basic house cleaning skills
  • Enumerate the major steps.
    • Clean the roof gutters
    • Winterizing your kitchen
    • Clean the car’s interior
    • Clean and organize the garage
    • Beat pollen and bugs

Present a step-by-step explanation of the process in chronological order, including cause-and-effect explanations as necessary.

  • Step 1: Clean the roof gutters
    • Define the Step
      • Clean your gutters the day after a light rain. You may be able to use a leaf blower to do the work.
    • Present the purpose or goal of current step
      • Unclog the roof gutter
    • Present chronological details of current step
      • Start clean drain outlets first
      • Use a narrow towel to scoop up muck and dispose it in garbage bag.
      • Tie the bag to your belt
      • Use the hose to remove loose silt
      • Move on to the next length of gutter
    • Explain how this step relates to the next step
      • It is the most exterior part of cleaning, after this, we can move inside the room and keep working
  • Step 2: Winterizing your kitchen
    • Define the Step
      • Maintenance on kitchen appliances and replacement if necessary
    • Present the purpose or goal of current step
      • Keep germs and grease buildup under control
      • Make everything smell fresher and look brighter
    • Present chronological details of current step
      • Clean both interior and exterior of refrigerator
      • Clean the stove
      • Clean and deodorize the dishwasher
    • Explain how this step relates to the next step
      • It is the biggest work to do inside the house so we do it first
  • Step 3: Clean the car’s interior
    • Define the Step
      • Clean not only the exterior but also the interior of your car
    • Present the purpose or goal of current step
      • Clean the mess inside your car and stock your ride with some things you might need for coming fall/winter
    • Present chronological details of current step
      • Clean and vacuum your car’s interior
      • Stock necessary stuff for coming cold seasons
      • Tackle the trunk
      • Keep your glove compartment organized
    • Explain how this step relates to the next step
      • Car is your daily mate so we clean it first, and then we can clean the garage
  • Step 4: Clean and organize the garage
    • Define the Step
      • Clean and organize the garage
    • Present the purpose or goal of current step
      • Release the space in the garage, get the clutter under control
    • Present chronological details of current step
      • Take an inventory of what your garage contains
      • Make a list of things you are planning to pitch
      • Pull everything our of the garage into a free field
      • Throw away things you don’t need
      • Bring things back in an organized way
  • Step 5: Beat pollen and bugs
    • Define the Step
      • Beat the pollen and bugs
    • Present the purpose or goal of current step
      • Get your house clean from dust, dander and mold
    • Present chronological details of current step
      • Clean bedding
      • Clean carpets
      • Clean drapes and blinds
      • Clean heater and air conditioner
      • Give your pets a bath
      • Clean moist surfaces
